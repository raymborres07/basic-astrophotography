The "Diskarte" Cheat Sheet

1. Pre-Flight Checklist

  • Moon Phase: Shoot only when illumination < 20% or after moonset.
  • Bortle Scale: Aim for Bortle 4 or lower for best Milky Way clarity.
  • The Dew Hack: Use a chemical hand warmer (kairo) to prevent lens fog.

2. Hardware Hacks

  • Remote Shutter: Use wired earphone volume buttons to avoid screen touch.
  • Focusing: Zoom in 10x on a bright star in Live View; minimize the dot size.
  • Steady Base: If no tripod, use a beanbag or your camera bag on a flat rock.

3. The 500 Rule Equation

To prevent star trails caused by Earth's rotation:

500 ÷ Focal Length = Max Exposure (s)

4. Post-Processing Tips

  • White Balance: Set to ~3200K (Tungsten) to kill city light orange.
  • Lightroom: Use HSL Orange Luminance to selectively dim light pollution.
  • Vibrance: Boost Vibrance over Saturation to protect natural star colors.
