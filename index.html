1. Hardware: Phones vs. Cameras

Smartphones (Zero-Budget)

Phone sensors are tiny and fixed-aperture. High ISO generates massive noise. The Fix: Shoot in RAW and stack images.

DSLRs & Mirrorless (Enthusiast)

Large sensors keep images clean at ISO 3200+. True wide-angle lenses keep stars sharp. Bottleneck: Cold weather kills batteries fast.

2. Planning & Weather

  • Clear Outside: Breaks down Low, Med, High cloud cover.
  • PAGASA: Localized rain band cross-referencing for Philippines.
  • LightPollutionMap: Find Bortle 4 or lower areas.
  • Stellarium: See exactly where the Milky Way will rise.
  • Moon Phase: Shoot only when illumination is < 20%.

3. Capture Apps (RAW Focus)

  • Lightroom Mobile: Built-in "Pro" camera shoots true RAW (DNG) for free.
  • Open Camera (Android): Completely free/open-source; lock focus to infinity.
  • Intervalometer (Android): Auto-clicker for hands-free stacking capture.

4. Post-Processing Stack

  • Sequator: Best free stacking software for beginners (Windows).
  • DeepSkyStacker: Complex but mathematical; for pros using mounts.
  • Lightroom Mobile: Best for color correction and neutralizing city haze.
  • Darktable: Free, open-source alternative to Lightroom for laptops.
